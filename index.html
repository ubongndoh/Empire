Built For Satisfactory Goods and Services

This Empire was established in November, 2018 after a two years period
of incubation, to become one of the country’s premier Products
and Service Empire, offering high quality and nutritious products and services to people around Nigeria and subsequently start exportation
to other African countries.

Charmarg Empire comprises of four major business activities which includes food processing,clothing, cosmetics and business consultancy.

The arm of CHARMARG EMPIRE that is into food processing and marketing is known as HANSMER FOODS, which produces Hansmer multi-grain cereal, a Afro-tropical food that is made with the sole aim of reducing malnutrition and hunger in the society through the use of nutritious grains and legumes such as ACHA, MILLET, and SOYBEANS.

The CLOTHING department of CHARMARG EMPIRE is into promoting the African culture through making and marketing of Africa fabrics such as Adire, Kampala, Nigeria Wax etc.

The BEAUTY arm of the Empire is into the production of hair products that solves the problem of “alopecia”. We have a range of hair products such as neem oil, neem hair cream, shampoo and conditioner which gives your hair 100% guarantee in replenishing and helping your hair grow the way you desire.

Services We Provide
